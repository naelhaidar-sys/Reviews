Client Recovery Bonus Calculator

Target-based model with no brackets. Each month, management sets a target per metric. If the employee reaches the target, he receives the full amount for that metric. If he reaches half the target, he receives half the amount. Each metric is capped at its assigned max. The total monthly cap is $550.
